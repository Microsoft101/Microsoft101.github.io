Chicken Burger

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken Pattey 1
Cheese 2
Tomato 1
burger Patty 2
lettuce 2 leaves
Ketchup -
mayo sauce -

Step 1: Take the top burger patty and put the two lettuce leaves on it

Step 2: Take the tomato peice and put it on top of the lettuce

Step 3: After the tomato take the 2 cheese and adjust it in the burger

Step 4: Then take the ketchup and pour it one the cheese as per your liking.

Step 5:Take the Chicken Patty next, and put it on the top of the ketchup.

Step 6: If you would like mayo then pour some of it on the Chicken Patty.

Step 7: Then put the the last Burger patty on the end.

Step 7: Lastly, heat the burger in a saucepan.

Eat Happily !!!!!

Pizza

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Pepperoni As per your requirement
Melted Cheese As per your requirement
Tomato As per your requirement
Ketchup or/and mayo As per your requirement
Chicken As per your requirement
Dough half KG
olives As per your requirement
Jalapenos(optional) As per your requirement

Step 1: Take the dough and mold it in the shape of a circle

Step 2: heat the pizza dough for 10 minutes. Now your Base is ready.

Step 3: after making the base pour 3/4 of your melted cheese all around the pizza. Do the same for ketchup

Step 4: after Doing that, add all the other ingrediants randomly across the pizza.

Step 5:After adding the ingrediants put your pizza in the oven and let it Bake for 10 minutes.

Step 6: Lastly add other ingrediants as per your taste.

Fries

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
potatoes 1 KG
Flour 1/4 KG
Masala As per your taste
Oil 1/4 KG

Step 1: Cut the 1 KG of patatoes in thin slices.

Step 2: put the potatoes in a bowl and pour the flour in it.

Step 3: Mix the cut slices in the flour.

Step 4: Heat the oil on a stove and fry the potatoes in it.

Step 5: Enjoy your fries with masala and/or with other ingrediants such as Ketchup or Mayo
